Projects

Assistive Smart Glasses for the Visually Impaired (Raspberry Pi + GPS)

A lightweight wearable solution designed to support mobility and awareness by detecting obstacles and potholes in real walking environments, with reading support and integrated GPS capability. Implemented on Raspberry Pi with emphasis on portability and real-world responsiveness.

Fruit Spoilage Detection Robot Arm

A robotic arm project focused on detecting spoiled fruit for quality-control workflows. Integrates perception with robotic behavior to improve consistency in identifying defective items.

IoT Hardware System for PlayStation Stores (Full Control)

A hardware-driven IoT control solution designed for operational use in PlayStation stores, enabling structured and reliable control of the environment through a centralized approach.

Remote-Controlled Factory Carrier Cart

A goods carrier platform designed for factory environments, controlled via remote operation and built with an operational mindset: stable motion, predictable response, and practical deployment suitability.

Smart Furniture (Mechatronics Integration)

A mechatronics-oriented smart furniture concept integrating sensing, actuation, and embedded control to enhance usability and automation.
